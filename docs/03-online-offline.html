Reader online and offline states

Overview 1 · Quiescent state 2 · QS variable 3 · Online / offline 4 · Deferred FIFO

A reader about to make a blocking call would stall the writer forever if it stayed online. So before blocking it goes offline: its slot is marked CNT_OFFLINE and the writer's check skips it entirely.
